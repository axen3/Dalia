Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping, shipping, and returns.

Shipping & Delivery

  • How long does shipping take?
    Standard shipping: 3–7 business days. Express: 1–2 days.
  • Do you ship internationally?
    Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. Duties may apply.
  • Is shipping free?
    All orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping.

Returns & Refunds

  • What is your return policy?
    30-day return window. Items must be unused with tags.
  • How do I start a return?
    Log in → Orders → Select item → Request Return.
  • Who pays return shipping?
    We provide a prepaid label for defective items.

Payments & Security

  • What payment methods do you accept?
    Credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay.
  • Is my payment information safe?
    Yes. We use 256-bit SSL encryption.

Product Questions

  • How do I know my size?
    Check our size guide on each product page.
  • Are colors accurate?
    Colors may vary slightly due to screen settings.
